# Privacy Policy for [polyhermes]

Last updated: [06/15,2026]

[polyhermes] is a Discord bot used to connect Discord messages to a Hermes Agent workflow.

## Information We Collect

When you interact with the bot, we may process or store:

* Your Discord user ID
* Your Discord username or display name
* Server, channel, and message metadata
* Message content you send to the bot
* Commands or prompts you send to the bot
* Files or attachments you choose to send to the bot
* Logs needed to debug errors, improve reliability, and maintain the bot

If the bot is connected to a local Hermes Agent, Claude, or other AI model provider, your messages may be sent to that system so the bot can respond.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

* Respond to your Discord messages and commands
* Run requested agent tasks
* Maintain bot functionality
* Debug errors and improve reliability
* Prevent abuse or unauthorized use

## Trading Bot and Local File Access

If the bot is connected to a local trading bot project, it may read files, logs, reports, or analysis outputs only when authorized by the operator. The bot is intended for paper trading analysis and development support only.

## Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared with third-party services only as needed to operate the bot, such as Discord, Hermes Agent, Claude/Anthropic, or other configured AI/model providers.

## Data Retention

We keep logs and message-related data only as long as needed for operation, debugging, safety, and development. Data may be deleted upon request when reasonably possible.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to protect bot tokens, API keys, logs, and user data. Users should not send sensitive personal information, passwords, private keys, or financial account credentials to the bot.

## Children

This bot is not intended for users under the minimum age required to use Discord.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
